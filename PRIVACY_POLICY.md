# Privacy Policy for Trave Social

**Last Updated:** December 21, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Trave Social ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, username, password
- **Profile Information:** Bio, profile picture, location, website
- **Content:** Posts, photos, comments, messages, stories
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates when you create posts or go live (with your permission)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App features used, time spent, interactions
- **Log Data:** IP address, browser type, access times
- **Location Data:** Approximate location based on IP address

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Social Media:** When you sign in with Google, Apple, TikTok, or Snapchat, we receive basic profile information (name, email, profile picture)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain our services
- Create and manage your account
- Enable social features (posts, comments, messages, live streaming)
- Show your location on maps (with your permission)
- Send notifications about activity on your account
- Improve our app and develop new features
- Prevent fraud and ensure security
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 Public Information
- Your profile (name, bio, profile picture) is public
- Your posts, comments, and stories are visible to other users
- Your location is shown on the map when you create posts (if you choose)

### 4.2 With Other Users
- Direct messages are private between you and the recipient
- Live stream viewers can see your video and location

### 4.3 With Service Providers
- **Firebase (Google):** Hosting, authentication, database, storage
- **Agora:** Live video streaming
- **Google Maps:** Location services

### 4.4 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We never sell your personal information to third parties.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- **Storage:** Your data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud) servers
- **Security:** We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS, TLS)
- **Retention:** We keep your data as long as your account is active
- **Deletion:** You can delete your account and data at any time

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Correction:** Update incorrect information
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and data
- **Portability:** Export your data
- **Objection:** Opt out of certain data processing
- **Withdraw Consent:** Stop sharing location or other optional data

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers in different countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

- Improve performance

For users in the EU/EEA, we process your data based on:
